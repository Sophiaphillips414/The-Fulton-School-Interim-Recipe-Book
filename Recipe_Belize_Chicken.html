Recipe #1 - Belize Chicken

Ingrediants
2 lbs chicken thighs (ish)
1 tbsp lime juice
2 tbsp soy sauce
cooking oil
1/2 tsp cumin
2 tbsp red recado
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 an union (1/2 cup diced)
1 inch of ginger minced
3 cloves garlic (1 tbs minced)
1 whole chopped bell pepper
1 can diced tomato
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 quart chicken broth
Thyme + cilantro sprigs, 2-4 each
salt + pepper to taste
Instructions
Season chicken with lime juice, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Set aside for 10 ish minutes, then sear in a little oiluntil each side is brown. Set aside, In the pot, add the parika, cumin, and red recado, and cook over low heat until fragrant. add oil and union, and turn up the heat slightly. Cook until unions are slightly translucent, then add garlic and ginger. When the garlic has lightly browned, add pepprs and tomatos, and cook for a few minutes. Add chicken and thyme and pour enough broth to cover everthing, then cover and cook at a low heat for about an hour, until chicken is tender. Add in salt and pepper to taste, and the cilantro, and serve with beans and rice.
