Malva Pudding

Puddng Ingredients
1 egg
1 cup sugar
1 cup cake flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 tablespoon apricot jam
1 tablespoon vinegar
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons butter
Sauce Ingredients
1 cup milk
3 tablespoons sugar
3 tablespoons butter
2 teaspoons vanilla
Whipped Cream Ingredients
1 cup whipping cream
2 tablespoons powdered sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
Ingrediant Name and Measurment here
Instructions
Add cream butter, egg and sugar together into a mixer Add the rest of the pudding ingredients and mix until combined. Pour into an oven proof dish and bake for 35 minutes. Meanwhile, whip the whipped cream ingredients together. In a saucepan, add the sauce ingredients and stir until warm. Pour over the hot pudding, and serve with whipped cream.
What went well
Another easy dish to make, you don’t need much to make it just a lot of time.
What didn’t go well
We rushed ourselves making the sauce for the pudding and messed up a little bit because of it.
Recommendations
You should make the sauce a few minutes before so it is still warm but will be done when the pudding is finished baking so you aren’t rushing it. You can also buy whipped cream or make it using heavy whipping cream and sugar.
